Introduction

Welcome to the MCP Directory ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by Forty Two Technologies, Inc. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Don't Collect

We want to be clear that the MCP Directory:

  • Does not collect personal information from visitors
  • Does not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Does not log IP addresses or browsing behavior
  • Does not require user accounts or registration
  • Does not collect payment information

Information About Listed Servers

The MCP Directory only displays publicly available information about MCP servers. This information includes:

  • Server names and descriptions
  • Features and capabilities
  • Public URLs and documentation links
  • Any other information voluntarily submitted for public display

Server Submissions

If you submit a server to be listed in our directory:

  • We only collect the information you voluntarily provide about your MCP server
  • All submitted information is intended for public display on our directory
  • We do not verify or take responsibility for the accuracy of submitted information

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, including the MCP servers listed in our directory. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Data Security

While we do not collect personal information, we implement reasonable security measures to protect the integrity of our website and the public information displayed.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect or display information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will post the revised policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
